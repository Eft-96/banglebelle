Weaving Dreams in Gold & Glass

A journey from the heart of Bengal to your wrists.

The Beginning

BangleBelle started with a simple yet profound realization: the ancient art of traditional bangle making in Bengal was slowly fading. Mass production was replacing the soulful touch of the artisan.

We set out on a mission to revive this heritage. Visiting remote villages, we connected with master craftsmen whose skills had been passed down through generations. Our goal was not just to sell bangles, but to tell their stories.

The Craftsmanship

Every BangleBelle piece is a labor of love. From melting the glass or metal to shaping the perfect circle, and hand-painting the intricate motifs, the process requires patience and precision.

We blend these traditional techniques with modern aesthetics, creating designs that are timeless yet contemporary. Whether it's a bridal set or everyday chic, you are wearing a piece of art.
